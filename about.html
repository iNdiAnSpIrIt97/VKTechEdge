03 : Bio

Academic Background

I completed my Bachelor's degree in Computer Science Engineering, where I developed a strong foundation in various aspects of computer science, including programming, algorithms, and data structures. My academic journey fueled my passion for technology and innovation.

Professional Experience

After graduating, I joined Tata Consultancy Services (TCS) as an Assistant System Engineer. During my two-year tenure at TCS, I worked on .NET and DevOps-related support projects. Despite gaining valuable experience, I found these projects unfulfilling and sought more engaging challenges. This led me to pursue my own development and learning activities parallel to my job.

Career Transition and Passion

Driven by my passion for web and app development, I decided to switch my career to focus on these areas. I worked at a product-based startup for one year, where I gained valuable insights into how projects are managed and executed in a dynamic environment. I am now dedicated to exploring new provisions of AI and other emerging technologies, constantly learning and evolving to stay at the forefront of the tech industry. My journey has been fueled by curiosity and a desire to create innovative solutions that make a difference. I am looking forward to advancing my career further and contributing to impactful projects in the technology space.
